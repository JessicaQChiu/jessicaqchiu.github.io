About Me

I'm a New Zealand business/IT graduate with professional experience working in the financial technology industry hoping to build a career in front-end development. I am open to opportunities in Vancouver or remote work in Canada, and have a work permit so I do not require employment sponsorship. Working as an operations analyst allowed me to build my analytical and writing skills, but I missed developing software which inspired me to take the leap and pursue web development.

My first experiences with technology included gaming on a boxy PC and when I learned that I could make a change directly to what I saw on the screen, you can bet that I customised my Neopets profile with a fairy wand cursor. Curiousity played a large part in my interest in tech and changing that single cursor design lead me down a path of developing interfaces for friends to use, programming a robotic arm, and volunteering to help spark that same enthusiasm in others.

I want to move forward by persistently developing my skills, diving into the knowledge of others, collaborating on cool projects, and providing my own perspective too. People have always been at the center of my work and previous roles. I would love the opportunity to be a part of a team that helps create a positive impact on how information is used, communicated, and shared - to help create solutions or paths so people can find them. I hope to collaborate and bring my perspective of inclusion, accessibility, and sustainability when developing strategies or products.

It's difficult to summarise my sincerity to enter this industry and how I will be diligent in my contributions - but it is easy for me to say that I would love a chance to work together with you to build something valuable.
